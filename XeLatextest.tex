			因为 $\rho = \lim\limits_{n\rightarrow\infty}\Big| \dfrac{a_{n+1}}{a_{n}}\Big| = \lim\limits_{n\rightarrow\infty}\dfrac{n\cdot3^n}{(n+1)\cdot3^{n+1}} = \dfrac{1}{3}$, \dotfill{}(2')
